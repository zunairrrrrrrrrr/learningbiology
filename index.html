About

A Passion for
Life Sciences

Welcome. I'm an Associate Professor of Biology and Biochemistry at Government Postgraduate College Sahiwal, with years of dedicated experience guiding students through the fascinating world of life sciences.

Through my Facebook page, Learning Biology / Biochemistry, I share educational content — detailed notes, diagrams, MCQs, and solved exercises — directly with students, free of charge, wherever they are.

My belief is simple: every student deserves clear, engaging, and accessible science education. Whether you're preparing for board exams or driven by sheer curiosity about how life works at the molecular level, this page is built for you.
